Swami Vivekananda

Arise,awake and donot stop until the goal is reached.

You have to grow from the inside out.

None can teach you, none can make you spiritual.

There is no other teacher but your own soul.

You cannot believe in God until you believe in yourself.

*Vivekananda has been credited with raising interfaith awareness and bringing Hinduism to a global platform in the 19th.*
